# Privacy Policy for LifeLens

**Last updated:** March 2026

LifeLens ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how LifeLens handles data when you use the application.

## 1. Information We Process

LifeLens may process the following types of information in order to provide its functionality:

### Camera Data
The app may access your device camera to capture images that are analyzed by AI models to help identify objects and provide assistance.

### Voice Input
If voice interaction features are enabled, the app may process audio input to understand user requests and generate responses.

### Usage Information
Basic usage information may be collected to help improve app functionality and performance.

## 2. How We Use the Information

The information processed by LifeLens is used solely to:

- Provide AI-powered object recognition and assistance
- Generate responses and explanations
- Improve app functionality and reliability

LifeLens **does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties for advertising purposes**.

## 3. Data Storage

LifeLens does **not permanently store personal images, voice recordings, or identifiable personal data** unless explicitly stated.

Most data is processed **temporarily during app usage** and is discarded after the request is completed.

## 4. Third-Party Services

LifeLens may use third-party AI services or cloud providers to process requests and generate responses. These services may temporarily process data required to deliver results.

These providers may include:

- AI model providers
- Cloud infrastructure providers

Data shared with these services is limited to what is necessary to perform the requested task.

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the app. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

LifeLens is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the updated date.
